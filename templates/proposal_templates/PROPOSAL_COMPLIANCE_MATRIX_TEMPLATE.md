\## Compliance Status Definitions



| Status | Meaning |

|---|---|

| Compliant | DMS fully meets the requirement. |

| Partially Compliant | DMS meets the requirement with clarification, configuration or additional work. |

| Not Compliant | DMS does not currently meet the requirement. |

| Requires Information | More information is required from the client or proposal team. |

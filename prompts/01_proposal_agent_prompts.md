\# Proposal Agent Prompts



\## Prompt 1: Analyse Bid TOR



Use 01\_DMS\_PROPOSAL\_AGENT\_CORE. Analyse the uploaded TOR/RFQ/RFP and extract:

\- client name

\- bid number

\- closing date

\- scope

\- mandatory requirements

\- functional requirements

\- non-functional requirements

\- evaluation criteria

\- scoring model

\- required returnable documents

\- risks and missing information



Return a bid summary, compliance checklist and requirement extraction matrix.



\## Prompt 2: Prepare Proposal Pack



Use 01\_DMS\_PROPOSAL\_AGENT\_CORE. Based on the TOR and previous DMS proposal, prepare a complete proposal structure including:

\- executive summary

\- understanding of requirements

\- DMS solution response

\- implementation approach

\- data migration approach

\- training strategy

\- support strategy

\- testing strategy

\- scoring response

\- annexure list

\# Project Governance Agent Prompts



\## Prompt 1: Create Project Charter



Use 02\_DMS\_PROJECT\_GOVERNANCE\_AGENT\_CORE. Based on the approved proposal and TOR, create a DMS Project Charter with:

\- background

\- objectives

\- scope

\- out of scope

\- deliverables

\- governance structure

\- roles and responsibilities

\- milestones

\- risks

\- assumptions

\- approval section

\- needs analysis

\- system architecture

\- change management strategy

\- risk management plan

\- test and go-live plan

\- UAT scripts

\- support plan

\- training plan

\- stakeholder matrix

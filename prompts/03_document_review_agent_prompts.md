\# Document Review Agent Prompts



\## Prompt 1: Review Document for Compliance



Use 03\_DMS\_DOCUMENT\_REVIEW\_AGENT\_CORE. Review the uploaded document against the applicable frameworks:

\- GIAMA

\- FIDPM / LGFIDPM

\- PFMA / MFMA

\- GRAP

\- IFRS

\- mSCOA

\- PMBOK 7

\- ISO 9001

\- TOGAF

\- client policies and procedures



Produce:

\- executive review summary

\- compliance matrix

\- gap analysis report

\- findings register

\- improvement action plan

\- audit readiness assessment



\## Prompt 2: Review C-AMP



Use 03\_DMS\_DOCUMENT\_REVIEW\_AGENT\_CORE. Review the uploaded C-AMP against GIAMA, the C-AMP guideline, PFMA, GRAP, FIDPM and any uploaded client policy. Identify missing sections, compliance gaps, audit risks and improvement actions.
